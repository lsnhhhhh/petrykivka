The museum holds a notable collection of works by the most prominent masters of Petrykivka decorative painting — Tetyana Pata, Nadiya Bilokin, Fedir Panko, Vasyl Sokolenko, and their students and followers. Petrykivka decorative painting is one of the unique expressions of Ukrainian artistic culture, recognized far beyond the region.

The collection was used as a primary source for the major 2001 album of reproductions *Petrykivka*, jointly with works from the Dnipropetrovsk Historical Museum. No Petrykivka works are featured in the museum's permanent exhibition, but the museum regularly hosts thematic Petrykivka exhibitions.
