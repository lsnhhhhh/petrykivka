Holds one of the largest collections of Petrykivka painting, dating back to 1913. This collection served as the foundation for many albums of reproductions published by *Mystetstvo* publishing house, including the largest and most representative album, *Petrykivka Painting* (2015).

Only a small portion of the collection is on permanent display — primarily items from the Kyiv Experimental Ceramic-Art Factory by Marfa Tymchenko, Vira Klymenko-Zhukova, Hanna Pavlenko-Chernychenko, and Vira Pavlenko, along with individual paper-based paintings by Tetyana Pata, Nadiya Bilokin, Fedir Panko, Vasyl Sokolenko, Marfa Tymchenko, Hanna Samarska, Volodymyr Hlushchenko, Andriy Pikush, and Nina Turchyn.
