Established in December 2013 in one of the rooms of the Petrykivka House of Culture in the village center. The museum holds a large collection of works by Fedir Panko owned by the artist's family, including such well-known pieces as the *Legend of the Petrykivka Cherry* series and his portrait of Petro Kalnyshevskyi. The exhibition also features several works by his daughter, Valentyna Panko, who serves as the museum's director.
