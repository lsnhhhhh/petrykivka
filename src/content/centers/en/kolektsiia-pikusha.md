Includes a substantial number of paper-based Petrykivka paintings by older masters. In 2014, an illustrated catalog of works by Nadiya Bilokin, Yaryna Pylypenko, and Paraska Pavlenko was published, drawing on the private collections of Volodymyr Padun and Andriy Pikush — including 57 pieces from the latter's collection.
