Holds one of the largest collections of Petrykivka painting, dating back to 1913. The collection was used as a major source for the large album of reproductions *Petrykivka* (2001), published jointly with works from the Dnipro Art Museum.

The museum's permanent exhibition on the second floor includes several display cases devoted to Petrykivka painting, featuring mainly items from the Petrykivka Painting Factory, the Experimental Workshop of Petrykivka Painting, and the *Petrykivka* Folk Art Center, along with several paper-based paintings and individual large works by Fedir Panko.
