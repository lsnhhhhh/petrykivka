The private collection of Ihor Lisnyi, a promoter and collector of Petrykivka painting, assembled since 2013. The collection combines works by masters with artifacts from the history of the art form: paintings and drawings, painting on wood (vases, plates, boards), and porcelain by the "Kyiv group" — including some of the earliest examples of Petrykivka painting on porcelain.

Among the represented authors are works by Halyna Nazarenko, Nina Turchyn, Andriy Pikush, Vira Pavlenko, Hanna Pavlenko-Chernychenko, Vasyl Sokolenko, Volodymyr Hlushchenko, Fedir Panko, Hanna Samarska, and many other masters. A separate part of the collection consists of rare publications on Petrykivka painting and its study, books decorated with the painting, as well as postcards and other printed materials from 1936 to the present — a valuable source of information on the history of the art form.
