Valentyna Milenko (born April 22, 1957, Balivka) is a master of Petrykivka painting and Honored Master of Folk Art of Ukraine (2014). She is a member of the National Union of Artists of Ukraine (since 1994) and the National Union of Folk Art Masters of Ukraine (since 2001).

## Education

She graduated from Dnipropetrovsk Technical Communications School No. 6 (1975) and Petrykivka painting courses (1989, teacher Valentyna Sokolenko).

## Work

She worked at the ["Druzhba" factory](/en/centers/fabryka-petrykivskyi-rozpys) (1978–1982, 1986–1990); since 1992 at the [Petrykivka Folk Art Center](/en/centers/tsentr-narodnoho-mystectva). Since 2011 she has led a Petrykivka painting club at Balivka Secondary School. She paints in gouache and egg tempera; her favorite subjects are flowers and birds. Major works: the panels "Bouquet" (1988), "Bird Gossip" (1991), "Lilac" (2001), "Irises" (2002), "Field Sonnet" (2014).

## Exhibitions

She has exhibited in all-Ukrainian and international shows since 1988. Solo exhibitions: Petrykivka, Dnipropetrovsk, Odesa, Kyiv, Toronto (2007), and Berdiansk.

## Collections

Her works are held in the [Zaporizhzhia Art Museum](/en/centers/zaporizkyi-khudozhnii-muzei), the [Dnipro Art Museum](/en/centers/dniprovskyi-khudozhnii-muzei), and the Ukrainian National Museum in Chicago.
