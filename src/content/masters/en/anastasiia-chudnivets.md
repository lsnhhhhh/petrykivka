Anastasiia Chudnivets (born March 10, 1988, Petrykivka) is a master of Petrykivka painting and an art historian, holding a PhD in art history (2020). She is the daughter of master painter [Valentyna Panko](/en/masters/valentyna-panko), granddaughter of the classic Petrykivka master [Fedir Panko](/en/masters/fedir-panko).

## Education

She graduated from the [Petrykivka Children's Art School](/en/centers/petrykivska-dytiacha-khudozhnia-shkola), Kryvyi Rih Pedagogical University (2010), and Kryvyi Rih University (2014).

## Work

She has exhibited Petrykivka painting abroad: Brasília (2011), UNESCO Headquarters in Paris (2013), Zagreb (2014), Vilnius (2017–2019), Sofia, and Plovdiv (2017).

Major works: "Char-zillia," "Yellow Asters" (2014), the diptych "Kupala Motifs" (2015), "Spring" (2019), "Motherhood" (2021).

## Research

She researches the distinctive character of Petrykivka painting and the work of [Fedir Panko](/en/masters/fedir-panko) and [Nina Turchyn](/en/masters/nina-turchyn). She is the author of the video-performance series "Problems of Petrykivka" (2020–2021) and scholarly articles on kitsch in amateur Petrykivka painting and Petrykivka ornament in graphic design.
